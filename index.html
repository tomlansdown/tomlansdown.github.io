About me

I’m Tom, a motion designer originally from Edinburgh, where I developed an eye for detail and a love of storytelling.

Specialising in motion design, I’ve gained experience in branding, animation and illustration and where they fit in the creative process. My focus recently has been on exploring different stages of the production workflow: crafting, developing and storyboarding ideas, whatever the medium.

With almost 5 years of agency experience, I make snappy motion graphics that are not only visually appealing but also communicate a clear message. Whether it's for brand videos, social media content or digital campaigns, visual storytelling is at the heart of what I do.
