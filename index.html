I'm a sketcher, designer and animator.

 

While studying graphic design I started to develop my skills in animation

and illustration. Since starting work, I have been learning the ropes in delivering

what the client is looking for, whilst also actively improving my own skills.

 

Recently, I have been experimenting with 3D animation and modelling.

I'm keeping myself busy exploring dynamics and realistic texturing.
